Welcome to the Settlers of Catan board Generator V 0.1

Howdy! Welcome to the "Settlers of Catan" board generator. This is an early version and will be upgraded in the future

I came up with the idea for the generator the first time I played Catan. Why shuffle and risk repetition, if you can leave the repetition to a randomizer

The spinning / highlighted tile is the tile where the "A" Token should be placed to determine the dice values for each resource

Please leave feedback and let me know if you like it and what would you like to see next

To get started, simply press the "Build Board" button below.
